It’s Always Sunny in Belgrade

Belgrade is a beautiful city located on the confluence of the Danube and Sava rivers. It is a Global City with a rich cultural heritage. Belgrade features diverse architecture, a charming Old Town district, beautiful churches and temples, many museums and important historical monuments.

The city is also famous for its vibrant nightlife. Many clubs and river barges are open until dawn and can be found throughout the city. There are rarely any significant language barriers and visitors always talks about the friendly atmosphere.

The Belgrade Faculty of Medicine

EMSA Belgrade’s base of operation is located near the heart of the city, within the Faculty of Medicine.

The University of Belgrade is one of the oldest universities in the region. The origin of the University of Belgrade can be tracked down to the beginning of the 19th century, when Dositej Obradović founded the College in 1808. Today, University of Belgrade is amongst the top 400 universities in the world, according to the most recent ranking carried out by the Shanghai Jiao Tong University, better known as the Shanghai List.

The Faculty of Medicine is one of the oldest faculties of the University of Belgrade. It was founded on December 9, 1920. Nine decades later, with 37,225 MD, 3,529 magister and 2,626 PhD obtained degree, the Faculty of Medicine is the leading institution for medical educational in Serbia and a significant player in regional terms.

Numerical data show that about 40% of Serbian medical students are taught at the Faculty, that two-thirds of clinical specialists and subspecialists diplomas are from the Faculty, and that over 60% of biomedical research projects funded by Ministry of Science are carried out at the Faculty.
